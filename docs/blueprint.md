# **App Name**: CasalCash

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure access for both users (Fabão and Tati) with real-time data synchronization across devices.
- Expense Logging: Easy recording of occasional and recurring expenses, indicating payer (Fabão or Tati) and split type (individual or shared).
- Expense Categorization: Categorize the expenses by type
- Loan Management: Record and manage loans between Fabão and Tati, including installment tracking and records of who purchased which item.
- Monthly Closing: Functionality to close each month and generate a consolidated financial report at the beginning of the next month.
- Balance Calculation: Calculate the total amount each user owes, incorporating monthly expenses and loan installments.
- Debt Reconciliation Tool: AI-powered feature to reconcile debts, showing a clear status of what each person owes or is owed, adjusting for multiple transactions using financial modeling to optimize clarity and fairness. The app considers all debts to produce statements such as 'Quanto eu devo para ela' and 'Quanto ela me deve'

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm coral (#FF7F50) to evoke a sense of comfort and security in financial management.
- Background color: Light peach (#FAEBD7), a desaturated version of coral, creating a soft, inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#DAA520), analogous to coral, to highlight important actions and elements, giving a sense of value.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font, conveys reliability and competence.
- Use simple, clear icons to represent expense categories and actions.
- A clean and intuitive layout, optimizing readability and ease of use.
- Subtle transitions to indicate financial reconciliation
